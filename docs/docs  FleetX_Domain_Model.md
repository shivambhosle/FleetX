# FleetX Domain Model

Version: 1.0

---

# 1. Overview

FleetX is designed around a shipment-centric logistics domain. Every business process revolves around fulfilling shipment requests using organizational resources such as vehicles, drivers, and dispatch operations.

The domain model identifies the core business entities and their relationships before translating them into a relational database.

---

# 2. Core Business Entities

The following entities form the core domain of FleetX:

## Organization

Represents a logistics company using FleetX.

---

## User

Represents a person who can access the platform.

Examples:

- Company Administrator
- Fleet Manager
- Dispatcher
- Driver
- Maintenance Manager

---

## Role

Defines user permissions within the organization.

---

## Customer

Represents the client requesting transportation services.

---

## Shipment

Represents a transportation request created by a customer.

A shipment contains:

- Pickup Location
- Delivery Location
- Priority
- Weight
- Volume
- Status
- Delivery Deadline

---

## Dispatch

Represents the operational planning process responsible for assigning resources to shipments.

---

## Vehicle

Represents a fleet asset used to transport shipments.

---

## Driver

Represents a person responsible for operating a vehicle.

---

## Trip

Represents the execution of a shipment.

A trip connects:

- Shipment
- Vehicle
- Driver

---

## Fuel Log

Stores fuel consumption records for vehicles.

---

## Maintenance Record

Stores preventive and corrective maintenance history.

---

## Compliance Document

Stores documents related to vehicles and drivers.

Examples:

- RC
- Insurance
- Permit
- Fitness Certificate
- Driving License

---

## Notification

Represents operational alerts generated by the system.

---

## Report

Represents generated operational summaries and analytics.

---

# 3. Entity Relationships

Organization
│
├── Users
├── Vehicles
├── Drivers
├── Customers
└── Shipments

Customer
│
└── Creates Shipment

Shipment
│
└── Generates Dispatch

Dispatch
│
├── Assigns Vehicle
└── Assigns Driver

Vehicle
│
├── Executes Trips
├── Fuel Logs
├── Maintenance Records
└── Compliance Documents

Driver
│
├── Executes Trips
└── Compliance Documents

Trip
│
├── References Shipment
├── Uses Vehicle
└── Uses Driver

Reports
│
└── Generated from Operational Data

Notifications
│
└── Generated from Business Events
